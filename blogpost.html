Blogs on Nalanda University

Nalanda University is an ancient university in India, it is over 800 years as a major center for buddhist learning, drawing scholars from across Asia.
It was destroyed around 1193 by Bakhtiyar Khilji and was later revived in modern times, leading to the establishment of a new Nalanda University in 2010 as an international, postgraduate, research-intensive istitution.

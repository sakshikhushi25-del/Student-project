Kaddu ka Halwa

Ingredients:

For making this wonderful recipe, we have to take these ingredients:-

  1. 1 Lauki
  2. 500g milk
  3. 1 spoon ghee
  4. 200g sugar
  5. 50g Dry fruits

After taking this firstly grate the lauki and then fry it in pan with ghee in medium flame.
then pour the milk in it and sugar together and then roast utill all the milk dries up.
After that the dry fruits mix in it and leave it for a while to cool down.Then the recipe is ready.
